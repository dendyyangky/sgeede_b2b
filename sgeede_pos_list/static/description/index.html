SGEEDE Custom Point of Sale

Enchance your point of sale with extra feature

SGEEDE Custom Point of Sale enchances your ui with features that streamlined the point of sale and while giving the option for users to change their ui and switch back and forth between the default ui or sgeede custom ui. This feature can be found and activated in the pos config menu.

List View for Your Point of Sale

Streamline and function focused, the list view allows point of sale operators to get straight to the product quickly. Removing the picture of the product to increase your screen real estate for the product information and allowing more products to be shown on the list at the same time.
